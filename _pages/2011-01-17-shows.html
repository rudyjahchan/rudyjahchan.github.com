Galacticast

Executive Producer, Writer, Lead Galacticast is an award-winning web series mashing up sci-fi, fantasy, horror, comics, video-games and internet memes to create hilarious geek-centric sketches. Since its’ launch on May 8, 2006, the series has had over 10 million views worldwide, received five Vloggie awards, and international mainstream media attention, including BBC News and The Hollywood Reporter.

A Comicbook Orange

Executive Producer, Writer, Host

A Comicbook Orange is a three-four minute weekly comic book web series featuring rapid-fire reviews and interviews with top industry creators. Hosted by Casey McKinnon, co-written by Rudy Jahchan and directed by Josh Allard, the team packages the series with custom special effects, creative intros and outros, and promotes interactivity with their audience.

Filmed primarily at the 8Bit Brownies Inc. studio, as well as on location at Meltdown Comics and various conventions, the show first launched on May 10, 2006 as a segment on Galacticast and later spun off into it’s own series on June 6, 2007.
