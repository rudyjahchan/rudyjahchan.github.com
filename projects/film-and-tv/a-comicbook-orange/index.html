A Comicbook Orange

A Comicbook Orange was a weekly show where we would review upcoming releases and classics, as well as interview their creators. Spinning out of a recurring Galacticast it found a home on Next New Networks before returning in it’s own self-hosted run. It won Rudy Jahchan the “Best Writing (Non-Fiction)” award at the Inaugural IAWTV Awards in 2012.
