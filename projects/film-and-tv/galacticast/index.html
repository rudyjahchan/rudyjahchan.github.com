Galacticast

Galacticast was a weekly home-brewed sci-fi parody web show filmed entirely out of our apartment in Montreal. Using some basic green-screen and compositiong we would produce a sketch show parodying the genre franchises we loved and the events of the days. Created during the early days of the online video boom as a “video podcast”, at it’s height it had over a million views a month.
